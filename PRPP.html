Welcome to PRPP!

What is PRPP? PRPP stand for ProgramRandom Practice Problems. These problems are designed to give you practice your programming skills at varying degress. Question may be taken or modified from ACSL problems. Visit the ACSL site here(ACSL is not affiliated or related to ProgramRandom) All problems taken from the ACSL site will have a disclaimer.

What are the rules? You may code in any language you would like! However, you must write your own code, you may not use outside modules. You may write your own modules. You "answer" must fit ALL possibilites. An answer may be posted here. You may only use the basic commands of your language for example, Python has a decimal to binary function, or bin(). You may not use this, you have to write it yourself. Specifics will be given in the actual problems.

Problems will be posted, approximately on a bi-weekly basis. Answers may, or may not be given. Enjoy!
